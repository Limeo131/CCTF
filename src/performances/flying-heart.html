The Flying Heart

《会飞的心》

A special festival performance by Zhaoyang — one night only.

About the Show | 关于本次演出

Zhaoyang, founder of Windmill Chinese Drama Club in 2011 and now an active performer on the Silicon Valley stand-up comedy scene, returns to Chicago with his touring show for a reunion in Hyde Park.

2011年风车剧社创始人、现活跃于硅谷脱口秀舞台的演员昭阳，将带着自己的巡演作品回到芝加哥，在海德公园完成一次充满情感意味的"重逢"。

This is not only a stand-up comedy special, but also a reconnection between an artist, a city, and the place where his youth once began. 这不仅是一场喜剧专场，也是一位创作者与这座城市、与自己青春起点之间的重新连接。
