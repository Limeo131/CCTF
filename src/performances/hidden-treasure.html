Hidden Treasure

《隐藏的宝藏》

Northwestern University Eighthday Theatre Club · 西北大学周八剧社

About the Production | 关于本次演出

Written by Stan Lai (赖声川) in 2018, Hidden Treasure is a play-within-a-play that reconstructs and extends Plautus's ancient Roman comedy A Pot of Gold. In the story, a theatre troupe accidentally discovers a wall covered in U.S. currency during a performance. A developer and a theatre manager each harbor their own motives, causing the themes of greed and suspicion from A Pot of Gold to reappear in reality.

《隐藏的宝藏》是赖声川在2018年创作的一部以戏中戏，是对古罗马剧作家普劳图斯《一坛金》的重构与续写。故事中的剧团成员在演出期间意外发现了一整面的美金墙，开发商、剧场经理各自心怀鬼胎，使得《一坛金》剧中关于贪婪与猜忌的主题在现实中重演。

In this absurd farce, "we" stand beneath a statue of the gods, moving between reality and illusion in search of what truly matters most in life. 在这个荒诞的闹剧中，"我们"矗立在神像下，穿梭于现实与虚妄，找寻何为生命中最重要的东西。

About Eighthday | 关于周八剧社

Founded in 2021, Eighthday Theatre Club is Northwestern University's first Chinese-language theatre troupe. Its name comes from "the eighth day" — an imaginary day that exists between Sunday and Monday — and symbolizes a space of imagination where Chinese-speaking students living abroad can express themselves freely and find release.

成立于2021年的周八剧社，是西北大学第一支华语剧团。它的名字来自"周日与周一之间，存在于幻想中的第八天"，也象征着剧团为身处异国的华语学生创造出的一个自由表达、自我释放的想象空间。
