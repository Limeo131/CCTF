An Act of Grace

《杀了我》

Dong Fang Drama Group · 东方话剧团

About the Production | 关于本次演出

Adapted from Canadian playwright John Muggleton's An Act of Grace, this production marks the work's world premiere of the Chinese version. The plot centers on three characters: two middle-aged men who have not seen each other for years suddenly appear at the home of a woman they do not know. If this is not a chance encounter, then what exactly is the "game" they speak of?

改编自加拿大剧作家John Muggleton的作品An Act of Grace，这是该作的全球中文版首演。剧情围绕三位人物展开：两位多年不见的中年男子，突然出现在一位陌生女子家中；如果并非巧遇，他们所说的"游戏"又究竟意味着什么？

Three people, three sets of calculations, and a dangerous endgame full of hidden currents gradually unfold and reverse again and again — until even the final line leaves suspense hanging in the air. 三个人、三种算计、一盘暗流涌动的残局，在不断推进中层层翻转，直到最后一句台词仍保留悬念。

Playwright in Attendance

Original playwright John Muggleton will travel to Chicago in person to attend this Chinese-language premiere and engage in conversations with audiences and creators during the festival. This is not only a production — it is a rare cross-cultural theatrical dialogue.

About Dong Fang Drama Group | 关于东方话剧团

Founded in 2005, Dong Fang Drama Group is a long-standing community theatre troupe rooted in Chicago. Its members — coming from all walks of life in the local community — have remained committed to theatrical creation and performance, staging dozens of Chinese and international classics as well as original works over twenty years.
