A Doll's House

《玩偶之家》

UChicago Windmill Chinese Drama Club · 芝加哥大学风车剧社

About the Production | 关于本次演出

One of Henrik Ibsen's most famous plays, A Doll's House is a landmark work in theatre history on the theme of women's awakening. It is being revived in this festival not only because it is a classic, but because the conditions it portrays still exist today: the forms of control hidden beneath the language of tenderness in intimate relationships, and the forces that appear to come from love and protection while in fact shaping and disciplining individuals.

《玩偶之家》是易卜生最著名的作品之一，也是戏剧史上"女性觉醒"主题的代表作。此次在戏剧节中再次呈现，不只是因为它经典，更因为它所呈现的处境至今仍然存在：亲密关系中那些披着温柔外衣的控制，那些看似出于爱与保护、实则不断塑造和规训个体的力量，依然普遍地发生在许多人的生活里。

In this production, Windmill invites audiences to reinterpret Nora from a contemporary perspective. She is not a traditionally "perfect," pure, flawless victim. She calculates, and she also uses her advantages to pursue what she wants. Her complexity, contradiction, and imperfection are precisely what make her feel like a real person.

About the Group | 关于剧团

UChicago Windmill Chinese Drama Club is one of the oldest and most influential Chinese-language theatre organizations at the University of Chicago. Since its founding, Windmill has continued to build stages between campus and city, using Chinese-language theatre to connect Chinese-speaking students, young creators, and local audiences. It is the primary initiator and main venue host of CCTF 2026.
