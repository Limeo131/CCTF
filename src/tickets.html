Performance Tickets | 演出票务

Hungry Panda Delivery
Exclusive official ticketing partner · 独家官方购票平台

All performance tickets are sold exclusively through the Hungry Panda Delivery platform. Search "CCTF" or "芝加哥中文戏剧节" directly in the Hungry Panda app to browse all events and purchase tickets.

1
Download the Hungry Panda app
Available on iOS and Android. Search "Hungry Panda Delivery" in the App Store or Google Play.
2
Search for CCTF events
Type CCTF or 芝加哥中文戏剧节 in the app's search bar to find all available performances.
3
Select your show and purchase
Choose your performance date and complete checkout. Your ticket will be delivered to your account.
4
Show your ticket at the door
Present your Hungry Panda ticket on arrival. Doors typically open 30 minutes before showtime.
Buy Tickets on Hungry Panda | 前往熊猫外卖购票
Scan to buy tickets | 扫码购票
Scan this QR code with your phone to go directly to the CCTF ticketing page on Hungry Panda.
用手机扫描二维码，直达熊猫外卖购票页面。

Workshops & Talks | 免费课程与对谈

All workshops and industry talks are free and open to the public. Advance registration is required. Seating is limited — first come, first served. 所有课程与对谈对公众免费开放，仅需提前预约，座位有限，先到先得。

check_circle
Free with registration | 免费，需提前预约
Register early to secure your spot. Each session ends with 15–20 minutes of Q&A.
All workshops are free. To ensure attendance, a $1 holding fee is collected at registration. If you can no longer attend, cancel at least 24 hours before the session for a full refund — your spot will be released to others. Seating is limited, register early.
所有课程均为免费，为保证出席，我们会收取 $1 占座费。如果报名后无法出席，活动开始前 24 小时取消预约即可全额退回，座位也会释放给其他人。座位有限，请提前预约。
