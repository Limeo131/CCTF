UChicago / Hyde Park

Main Festival Venue · 主会场

location_on FXK Theater (三楼), 5706 S University Ave, Chicago, IL 60637

The primary hub of CCTF 2026, Hyde Park hosts the bulk of productions, all workshops, and special events. Home base of the Windmill Chinese Drama Club, which has been building stages in this neighborhood for years.

SAIC / Loop

Downtown Chicago · 城市中心

location_on School of the Art Institute of Chicago, Chicago, IL (Loop area)

A downtown theatre space ideal for evening performances and city audiences. The Loop location makes it easily accessible for audiences who live or work in central Chicago, and gives CCTF a presence in the heart of the city's arts district.

Northwestern / Evanston

North Campus · 北部校园

location_on Northwestern University, Evanston, IL

Northwestern's Eighthday Theatre Club brings CCTF to the north side, connecting the Evanston campus community with Chinese-language theatre for the first time through the festival. The Evanston venue extends the festival's geographic reach beyond Chicago proper.

Events at this venue

Burr Ridge

Community Theatre · 社区剧场

location_on Dong Fang Drama Group Small Theatre, 360 Shore Drive, Burr Ridge, IL 60527

Home of the Dong Fang Drama Group, the Burr Ridge venue is where Chinese community theatre has been rooted since 2005. This is not a university stage — it's a community space built and maintained by working adults who have sustained their love of theatre for two decades.
