Acting Workshop

表演课程 · 从零基础到进阶表达

Two sessions for beginners and experienced performers. With Yuna (优娜).

Yuna (优娜)
Actor · Theatre & Film Creator

One of the few Chinese stage actors and theatre/film creators based in Chicago. Youna brings professional stage experience to accessible, grounded teaching that works for both complete beginners and experienced performers.

About the Workshop | 课程简介

Two acting workshops designed respectively for complete beginners and participants with prior experience. Starting from "What is acting, and what exactly does acting perform?", the classes guide participants through foundational theatrical concepts, acting terminology, and ensemble exercises, before moving into deeper training in character relationships, objectives, and dialogue.

两门表演课程，分别面向零基础参与者与有一定经验的学员。课程将从"什么是表演、表演到底在演什么"出发，带领参与者进入戏剧基础概念、表演术语、团队配合练习、小短剧练习，并进一步延伸到人物关系、人物目标、对白处理与角色理解等更深入的表演训练。

Topics Covered | 课程内容

  • What is acting and what does it perform
  • Foundational theatrical concepts and terminology
  • Ensemble exercises and short-scene practice
  • Character relationships and objectives
  • Dialogue work and character understanding
  • Q&A with Yuna
Each session reserves 15–20 minutes at the end for Q&A. Come with questions. The classes are held in a bilingual (English/Chinese) environment. 每节课最后预留15–20分钟Q&A时间。课程在双语（中英文）环境中进行。
