Directing Masterclass

导演大师课 · 如果只能教一节导演课

From script to stage — the full director's process. With Joel Willison.

Joel Willison
Theatre Director · Chicago

Chicago-based theatre director with extensive experience working across professional and academic productions. Joel brings a practical, process-driven approach to directing that demystifies the craft for newcomers.

About the Masterclass | 课程简介

From the moment a director first receives a script, how do they understand the text, develop an overall concept, collaborate with actors and creative departments, and ultimately bring the work to the stage? This masterclass offers an entry point for beginners while staying grounded in real creative practice.

从第一次拿到剧本开始，导演如何理解文本、建立整体构想、与演员和各创作部门合作、最终将作品带到舞台现场，这门课将提供一个面向初学者但又贴近实际创作流程的入口。

Topics Covered | 课程内容

  • Reading and interpreting a script
  • Building an overall directorial concept
  • Collaborating with actors and creative departments
  • From page to stage: the full process
  • Q&A with Joel Willison
No prior directing experience is needed. The masterclass is designed to give a real, honest picture of how a production takes shape — from the first read to opening night.
