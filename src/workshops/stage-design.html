Stage Design Masterclass

舞美大师课 · 舞台设计中的合作与取舍

From concept to realization — with Joonhee Park, scenic designer and Associate Professor at NEIU.

Joonhee Park
Scenic Designer · Associate Professor, NEIU

Chicago-based scenic designer and Associate Professor at Northeastern Illinois University. Joonhee has designed sets for Raven Theatre and numerous Chicago productions, bringing a rigorous, collaborative approach to every project.

About the Masterclass | 课程简介

Drawing on her work for Raven Theatre's Top Girls, Joonhee Park shares the full process of scenic design from concept to realization — discussing collaboration in creation, the importance of subtraction in design, and how to find the "just right" balance in artistic expression.

她将结合Raven Theatre的作品《Top Girls》分享舞美设计从概念到落地的全过程，讨论创作中的合作、设计中的"减法"、以及如何在艺术表达中找到"刚刚好"的状态。

Topics Covered | 课程内容

  • Scenic design from concept to realization
  • Collaboration between designers and directors
  • The art of subtraction in design
  • Case study: Top Girls at Raven Theatre
  • Finding the "just right" balance in artistic expression
  • Q&A with Joonhee Park
The masterclass is designed to be accessible to people who have never worked on a production, while still being substantive for those with experience. Come curious. 本次大师课面向所有人，无论你是否有舞美经验，都能找到有价值的内容。
