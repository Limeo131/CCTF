A Young Asian Actor's Journey

00后亚裔演员历程分享

Industry Talk with Thomas Tran — actor active at Goodman Theatre and Chicago stages.

Thomas Tran
Actor · Goodman Theatre & Chicago Stages

An emerging Asian actor active in Chicago theatres including Goodman Theatre. Thomas's career spans school applications, training, and professional auditions — navigating what it means to build a theatre career as a young Asian artist in the United States.

About the Talk | 对谈简介

Thomas Tran shares his experiences with school applications, auditions, and performance — giving audiences a closer look at how a young actor gradually enters the professional theatre system in the United States.

作为活跃于Goodman Theatre等芝加哥剧场的青年亚裔演员，Thomas Tran将分享自己的考学、试镜与表演经历，带大家了解一位年轻演员如何一步步进入专业剧场体系。

Topics Covered | 对谈内容

  • Navigating school applications for theatre programs
  • The audition process in professional theatre
  • Life as a working actor in Chicago
  • Experiences as an Asian actor in American theatre
  • Q&A with Thomas Tran
This is an open conversation, not a lecture. Bring your questions — especially if you are wondering what a career in theatre actually looks like from the inside.
