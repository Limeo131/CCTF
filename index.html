Connecting Campus and Community

校园与社区的桥梁

CCTF creates a vibrant space where university students and the broader Chinese community meet. We believe theatre is the ultimate platform for shared dialogue, bringing academic inquiry and local lived experiences together under one spotlight.

Program Highlights

节目亮点 | 精彩纷呈

Drama May 15-17

Stones, Sun, Red | 《石头，太阳，红色》

A poetic exploration of memory and the persistence of childhood dreams in an ever-changing landscape.

Classic Adaptation May 19-21

A Doll's House | 《玩偶之家》

Ibsen's masterpiece re-imagined through a contemporary Chinese lens, exploring gender roles and societal constraints.

No Exit | 《禁闭》

Hell is other people. A minimalist take on Sartre's existential classic.

Hidden Treasure | 《隐秘的宝藏》

A family mystery unfolds during a traditional festival celebration.

Kill Me | 《杀了多》

A dark comedy exploring the absurdities of modern office life and ambition.
