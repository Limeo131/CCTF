Connecting Campus and Community

校园与社区的桥梁

CCTF creates a vibrant space where university students and the broader Chinese community meet. We believe theatre is the ultimate platform for shared dialogue, bringing academic inquiry and local lived experiences together under one spotlight.

风起于青萍之末

在这座被称为风城的芝加哥，风从来不只是气候的描述，而是一种穿行于楼宇与人群之间的呼吸，一种时刻在场的力量。今年春天，我们想邀请你一起追风——用戏剧的方式。

Classic Drama May 17–19

A Doll's House | 《玩偶之家》

A woman who has been living like a "doll" ultimately chooses to leave her home in order to claim her dignity and freedom as a human being. ｜ 一个被当作"玩偶"活着的女人，最终选择离开家庭，去争取作为"人"的尊严与自由。

World Premiere · Chinese Version May 31

An Act of Grace | 《杀了我》

In what seems like a chance encounter, three people with hidden motives test, trap, and turn on one another, until the truth twists violently in the very last moment. ｜ 三个各怀心思的人在一场看似偶然的相遇中彼此试探、设局与反噬，直到真相在最后一刻彻底翻转。

No Exit | 《禁闭》

"Hell is other people." A minimalist take on Sartre's existential classic. ｜ "他人即地狱。"——对萨特存在主义经典的一次极简演绎。

Hidden Treasure | 《隐藏的宝藏》

An unexpected fortune lays bare the greed and absurdity of human nature. ｜ 一笔意外出现的财富，照出了人性的欲望与荒诞。
